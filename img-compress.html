How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compression tool makes it easy to reduce file sizes while maintaining visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = more compression)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep the original)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Improved SEO rankings
  • Saving storage space
  • Optimizing images for social media
